“an unforgettable week on the mountain”

The Ski Week is a school holiday program run by the Otago Ski Club for school children between 9 & 18 years.


It involves staying at the lodge for the week and attending twice daily skiing or boarding lessons run by NZSki Lessons, available for beginners through to advanced skiers and boarders.


Six hut parents supervise the children during their stay in the lodge, and arrange breakfast, dinner and evening activities.


A ski week is an amazing way to spend a week up on the mountain and many of the participants (and hut parents) come back year after year for the fun!


If you are interested in joining ski weeks please email skiweeks@otagoskiclub.org.
