Welcome


The Otago Ski Club is a sports club for skiers (and snowboarders), which has been based in the Otago region of New Zealand since it was first established in 1932.


The club operates “Joel Lodge”, a lodge situated at the top of Coronet Peak Road, a short walk from the bottom of the chairlift. The club offers what no hotel in the Queenstown Lakes District can: affordable, peak-side accommodation on Coronet Peak with the convenience of being only ten minutes walk from the base buildings!


The lodge operates during the winter ski season, and in recent years also over summer, providing accommodation for groups or individuals participating in outdoor recreation activities.


Be among the first on the mountain and rest in the knowledge that after the end of the day, you have only a short distance to walk or slide to your accommodation and aprés ski.


The lodge has cooking facilities that guests can use, and there are shared catered meals arranged by the caretakers on the busy winter weekends.


We’re a friendly, family-oriented club, and are always open to new members. If you’re planning on spending some time in Queenstown – either in Winter or Summer, give us a try.

Welcome to the Otago Ski Club


The Ski Season


Our season runs from one day prior to the opening of the Coronet Peak ski field until one day after it closes. During this time our caretaker is in attendance at Joel Lodge.


Catering


The Lodge will be self catering for all meals except during peak season catered weekends where Sat & Sun breakfast is provide. For catered weekend bookings please try and book by the prior Tuesday. Dates for catered weekends are advised by email and posted on the website.


Accomodation


• Provide your own sleeping bag and pillow slip (pillow provided)


• Bookings to be made via the website, Contact us for more information.


• Facilities include drying room, kitchen, lounge, dining room & ski repair room


• Sleeping quarters are large shared bunk rooms. There are two family rooms which sleep two adults and two chidren


• No luggage in bunkrooms (fire requirement) – lockers provided in changing rooms


• No food in bunkrooms, bunkrooms are silent rooms


• Everyone is expected to carry out duties – caretaker will advise you of these


• Sign in on the board on arrival


• Car parking is available on the left side of Vincent Lodge – do NOT bring your car down to the lodge to unload, you will likely get stuck!


• The Lodge is locked during the day – caretaker will provide security number


Other Information


• Fees can be found on the SKI WEEKS web page


• Club Events – Ski weeks for children in July holidays, Club Week and Club Weekend. Usually the first weekend in August. Details will appear on website.


• Summer usage of the Lodge is available


• AGM held at the end of March
